SHIPWRECK


Event time: // mins
DESCRIPTION:

Imagine you're in a ship. Only one life jacket is left. Either you or the captain can use it and save life. The participant is given with a role (say Donald Trump) by the Judge.

FOR AND AGAINST

Actions may speak louder than words. But that doesn't mean words aren't important.

DESCRIPTION:

  • The contestant is given with a topic (say money).
  • Initially he/she must speak FOR the topic i.e.: pro's of the topic.
  • Once the judge hits the bell, he/she must speak AGAINST the topic i.e.: cons of the topic.
  • Judge's decision will be final.
