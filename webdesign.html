<div>Sharpen your HTML and CSS and get ready for the ultimate battle of creation!</div>
This is a web designing event. The purpose of this event is to find some excellent and creative web developers among us and use their skills for the benefit of our team and the entire college. This event consists of three rounds to test your development skills and creativity at various depths.

<h1>Round 1(//Timing):</h1>

<p>MCQs</p>

<h1>Round 2(//Timing):</h1>

<p>Contestants will be provided with layout of a web page or a particular section of it and will be asked to write the code for the page. At the end of this round, we’ll shortlist the most skillful people who finish the given task and will be considered as winners (because creativity isn't necessary for developers).</p>

<h1>Round 3(//Timing):</h1>

<p>This round will test the creativity of the developers. They’ll be asked to design and develop a page in a given theme (For eg., Product showcase website). And their development doesn’t have to be limited just to one page. They can create as much pages as they want within the given duration. The page or the website which stands out will be declared winner. Jury's decision will be final.</p>
(Winners will be awarded with cash prize of //amount and an e-certificate).
